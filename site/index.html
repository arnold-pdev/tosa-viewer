TOSA shape sensitivities

Relative compliance sensitivity on faired NITO-3D surfaces. VC,rel is the fractional compliance change per unit boundary offset (negative = outward motion reduces compliance). Blue pulls inward, red pushes outward. Teal tint marks fixed BC patches. Arrows show NITO load anchors and force directions.
